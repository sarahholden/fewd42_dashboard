    Assignment 6

    Due July 10th at 11:59pm

    Your sixth assignment will be to get started on the HTML and CSS for your final project.

    Deliverable:

    Files for your final project (use the boilerplate provided in lesson 10.

    At this point, we want to see that you have spent 3-4 hours getting a start on your project.

    How to get started:

    • Don't be intimidated! Just start writing code.
    • Start with your HTML, just like we do in class.
    • Then start writing your CSS.
    • It may be helpful to pick 2-3 fonts and a 3-4 colors for a basic design guide.
    • At this point, feel free to use an ipsum generator for text and placeholder images.

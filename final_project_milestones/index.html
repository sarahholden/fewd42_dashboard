Deliverable:

Descriptive overview (a couple of paragraphs) along with some drawn wireframes for what you'd like to do for your final project.

In other words: using words and pictures, give us as good idea as possible what you'd like to make.

How to create your wireframes:

  • Option 1: use a tool like https://wireframe.cc/ for creating wireframes.
  • Option 2: Draw out wireframes and scan or take a picture of them
  • Option 3: Designer? Feel free to use Photoshop/InDesign/etc. to create wireframes
